Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who is taking my request?
Our platform is a marketplace of "Runners"—local experts, bilingual residents, and expats living across China. They provide "boots on the ground" support and understand the local systems. You aren't hiring a company; you are connecting with real people.
2. How much does a "Quest" cost?
Price is flexible! You can either post an "Offer" or leave it open for Runners to "Bid." This allows multiple runners to compete for your task, so you can choose the person and price that fits your budget best.
3. How do I pay, and is it safe?
Direct-Market: Pay your Runner directly. We do not handle funds or charge fees.

Escrow-Market: We act as a trusted middleman. We hold your payment securely and only release it after you confirm delivery. The Commissioner pays zero platform fees; commission (5-10%) and currency fees are deducted from the Runner's payout.
4. Can you find the "cheapest" price?
Our Runners act as your local advocate. They will search the market to find the most reasonable rates for you. If you have a specific store or website you prefer, just tell your Runner!
5. Can you help me find rare items?
If it exists, we’ll help you find it! From limited edition collectibles to specific local teas, our Runners love a challenge. For rare requests, a higher reward might be needed to attract the right Runner.
6. Professional translation or legal advice?
Most Runners are bilingual assistants. However, our community is open to everyone—including licensed professionals. If your task requires a certified expert, specify this in your post and verify their credentials before hiring.
7. Can you help me if I don’t live in China?
Yes! Many of our Commissioners are outside of China. We can help you coordinate future visits, inspect factories, or send gifts to loved ones living in China.
8. What if I am not happy with the result?
For Escrow tasks: You can open a dispute through our support team. We will step in as mediators to investigate and provide the best resolution within our power.

3rd Parties: While we can’t control shop policies, your Runner will act as your advocate to negotiate the best possible result.
